Artificial Intelligence Ethics

AI ethics is a branch of ethics specifically concerned with the moral implications and societal impact of artificial intelligence systems.

Key Principles

  • Fairness: AI systems should treat all individuals and groups equitably
  • Transparency: AI decision-making processes should be explainable and understandable
  • Privacy: Personal data should be protected and used responsibly
  • Accountability: Clear responsibility for AI system outcomes

As AI becomes more prevalent in society, addressing these ethical considerations becomes increasingly important for responsible AI development.
